## 2. Layer 1/Layer 2 Protocols Hiring

### Arbitrum
- **Hiring**: Yes, actively hiring in 2024
- **Tech Stack**: Solidity, Go, Rust, TypeScript
- **Roles**: Protocol engineers, security researchers, DevOps
- **Notes**: Offchain Labs team; one of the largest L2 ecosystems

### Optimism
- **Hiring**: Yes, active hiring
- **Tech Stack**: Solidity, Go, TypeScript, Rust (for OP Stack components)
- **Roles**: Protocol engineers, frontend, ecosystem development
- **Notes**: OP Stack is increasingly important; Bedrock upgrade experience valuable

### Base (Coinbase L2)
- **Hiring**: Yes, Coinbase continues hiring for Base
- **Tech Stack**: Solidity, Rust, Go, TypeScript
- **Roles**: Protocol engineers, developer relations, security
- **Notes**: Strong backing by Coinbase; OP Stack based

### zkSync (Matter Labs)
- **Hiring**: Yes, heavily hiring
- **Tech Stack**: Solidity, Zinc (Rust-based), Rust, Go
- **Roles**: ZK电路工程师, Solidity devs, protocol engineers
- **Notes**: Type 2 ZK-EVMs; demand for ZK knowledge is HIGH

### StarkNet (StarkWare)
- **Hiring**: Yes, active
- **Tech Stack**: Cairo (StarkWare's language), Solidity, Rust
- **Roles**: Cairo developers, protocol engineers, Rust engineers
- **Notes**: STARKs vs SNARKs; Cairo is a growing skill in demand

### Ethereum Core Development
- **Hiring**: Yes, via Ethereum Foundation and client teams
- **Teams**: Geth, Nethermind, Erigon, Lighthouse, Teku, Prysm
- **Tech Stack**: Go, Rust, C++, Python, Solidity (for EIPs)
- **Roles**: Protocol researchers, client developers, security engineers
- **Notes**: Vitalik and EF fund various teams; less "hiring" in traditional sense

## 5. Remote Crypto Dev Salary Ranges (2024-2025)

### Seniority Levels (Remote, Global)

| Level | Solidity/Smart Contract | Frontend DeFi | Protocol Engineer | Security/Audit |
|-------|-------------------------|---------------|------------------|----------------|
| **Junior (0-2 yrs)** | $80k - $130k | $70k - $120k | $90k - $140k | $90k - $150k |
| **Mid (2-4 yrs)** | $130k - $200k | $120k - $180k | $150k - $220k | $150k - $250k |
| **Senior (4-7 yrs)** | $180k - $300k | $160k - $250k | $200k - $350k | $220k - $400k |
| **Lead/Principal** | $250k - $450k+ | $200k - $350k | $300k - $500k+ | $350k - $600k+ |

### Compensation Breakdown

**Base Salary Components:**
- **US-based remote**: 20-40% premium over other regions
- **EU-based**: Competitive with US for senior roles
- **Asia-based**: 30-50% lower base, but often with token bonuses
- **Token/Equity**: 10-40% of total comp in protocol tokens (vested)

**Top-Tier Total Compensation (with tokens):**
- Senior Solidity at major protocol: $300k - $500k+ total
- Security researcher at leading audit firm: $400k - $600k+
- Quant/trading firm senior devs: $400k - $800k+ (bonus-heavy)

### Crypto vs Traditional Web3 Salaries

| Role | Traditional Web3 | Crypto-Native | Difference |
|------|------------------|---------------|------------|
| Senior Engineer | $150k - $250k | $200k - $350k | +30-40% |

| Protocol Engineer | N/A | $200k - $400k | Premium |

### LinkedIn Strategy
- Search: `"Solidity" OR "smart contract" OR "DeFi" developer`
- Filters: Remote, Global
- Follow: Protocol official pages for job posts
- Set job alerts for: "blockchain engineer", "crypto developer", "solidity"

### AngelList/Haystack
- **AngelList**: Many early-stage crypto startups
- **Haystack**: Curated crypto VC portfolio companies
- **Format**: Often startup equity + token comp

### Hidden Job Market (Not Publicly Posted)

1. **Protocol Discord/Telegram communities**
   - #jobs channels in dYdX, GMX, Uniswap Discords
   - Direct outreach to team members

2. **Twitter/X Networking**
   - Follow protocol founders and engineers
   - Engage with content; many roles filled via network

3. **Gitcoin Grants & Bounties**
   - Some protocols post bounties instead of full roles

4. **Conference Networking**
   - ETHGlobal, Devcon, Token2049, ETHDenver
   - Many hires made at hackathons

5. **Referrals**
   - 30-40% of crypto dev roles filled via referrals
   - Reach out to current employees on LinkedIn

## Key Takeaways & Recommendations

### Highest Demand Areas (2024-2025)
1. **ZK Developers** (Cairo, ZK circuits) — Premium salaries
2. **Rust Engineers** (Cosmos, Solana, some L2s)
3. **Smart Contract Security Auditors** — Severe shortage
4. **Protocol Engineers** (AMM, derivatives logic)

### Best Protocols to Target for Employment
1. **dYdX v4** — Cosmos migration complete, strong team
2. **Uniswap Labs** — Largest DEX, strong compensation
3. **zkSync/Matter Labs** — Top ZK-EVM, growth stage
4. **Arbitrum/Optimism** — Largest L2 ecosystems
5. **Aave/Compound** — Blue-chip DeFi, stable

### Salary Maximization Strategies
1. **Security/Audit specialization** commands 30-50% premium
2. **ZK knowledge** is increasingly valuable
3. **Remote for US companies** while living in lower-cost regions
4. **Token comp** negotiation at protocol-level companies

### Resources for Job Search
- Daily: Web3.career, Crypto Jobs List
- Weekly: DeFi Llama jobs board, Ethereum Foundation blog
- Community: Protocol Discords, Twitter, Gitcoin
