# Sakartvelo Defenders — Art Style Guide v3.0

## 🎨 Visual Philosophy
**"The Digital Textbook"** — A blend of historical illustration aesthetic and modern dynamic lighting.

## 🛠️ Core Principles
1. **Bold Definitions**: Every character and tower must have a **2px solid dark outline** (`#1A1A1A`).
2. **Proportions**: Humans follow a **10-unit scale** (e.g., 2 units head, 4 units torso, 4 units legs).
3. **Typography**:
   - **Titles**: *Mrgvlovani* (Ancient) and *Mkhedruli* (Modern) fonts.
   - **Body**: Serif fonts (e.g., Georgia, Times New Roman) for that historical book feel.
4. **Color Palette**:
   - **Main Accent**: "Sakartvelo Gold" (`#D4A017`).
   - **Secondary**: "Ancient Parchment" (`#F0E6D2`).
   - **Shading**: Cel-shaded (toon) look with **2 discrete shadow steps**.
5. **VFX**: Minimalist particles with gold/white glow effects.

## 🖥️ UI Layout
- **Minimalist HUD**: Semi-transparent, dark glassmorphic backgrounds.
- **Iconography**: Clean, line-art icons that match the 2px outline rule.
- **Feedback**: Smooth transitions and micro-animations for every button click.

## 🗺️ Level Design
- **Grid-Based**: 3D Isometric view.
- **Palette Shifting**: Each Era has its own distinct environment palette (e.g., Lush Green for Colchis, Desert Sand for Iberia).
